Hello, I'm a world traveling, pseudo wine connoisseur who likes to run and looking forward to making major career changes in 2017

Here's a snippet of my story

I'm orignally from Virginia but I've made my home in Metro Atlanta for the past 20 years. My background and education in the economics and finance field led me to a career in the telecommunications industry as a financial consultant. As rewarding as these 20 years have been, it is time to move on and embark on something different. I've always been intrigued by technology and the fact that it's an ever changing industry that requires you to stay on your toes!

So, I've decided that I'd like to learn how to code as an introduction to the everchanging world of technology. I've always been a problem solver and a creator of resolutions. So what could be more perfect than learning to code? I appreciate a challenge and learning something completely different certainly qualifies. It's a mental adjustment but it's also very exciting and I look forward to progressing and working towards my goal of changing careers.
